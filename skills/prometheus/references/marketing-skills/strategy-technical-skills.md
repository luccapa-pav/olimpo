# Paid Ads Strategy (cross-platform)

## Seleção de plataforma
- Google Ads: high-intent search, capturar demanda existente
- Meta: demand gen, produtos visuais, broad targeting
- LinkedIn: B2B, decision-makers, higher price points
- TikTok: younger demographics, viral creative

## Frameworks de copy
- PAS (Problem-Agitate-Solve)
- BAB (Before-After-Bridge)
- Social Proof Lead

## Targeting
- Google: keywords + audience layering + RLSA
- Meta: core (interests) → custom (website/list) → lookalike (1% best customers)
- LinkedIn: job function + seniority + company size

## Retargeting funnel
- Top: blog readers, video viewers → conteúdo educativo
- Mid: pricing/feature pages → case studies, demos
- Bottom: cart/trial → urgência, objeções, ofertas

## Budget
- Testing: 70% proven + 30% teste
- Scaling: +20-30% por vez, esperar 3-5 dias entre aumentos

# SEO Audit Framework

## Prioridade
1. Crawlability & Indexation (Google encontra e indexa?)
2. Technical (site rápido e funcional?)
3. On-Page (conteúdo otimizado?)
4. Content Quality (merece rankear?)
5. Authority & Links (credibilidade?)

## Checklist técnico
- robots.txt, XML sitemap, arquitetura (3 cliques do homepage)
- Core Web Vitals: LCP <2.5s, INP <200ms, CLS <0.1
- HTTPS, mobile-first, URL structure

## On-page
- Title tags: únicos, keyword no início, 50-60 chars
- Meta descriptions: únicos, 150-160 chars, CTA
- H1 único, hierarquia lógica (H1→H2→H3)
- Keyword cannibalization check

## E-E-A-T
- Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness

# Launch Strategy (ORB Framework)

## Canais ORB
- Owned: email, blog, comunidade (compound value)
- Rented: social media, marketplaces (velocidade, não estabilidade)
- Borrowed: guest content, collabs, influencers (credibilidade instantânea)

## 5 Fases de launch
1. Internal: feedback de early users amigáveis
2. Alpha: landing page + waitlist + MVP em produção
3. Beta: early access + marketing com teasers
4. Early Access: leak details, pesquisa quantitativa, throttle invites
5. Full Launch: self-serve signups, cobrar, anunciar em todos os canais

## Product Hunt
- Antes: build relationships, otimizar listing, estudar launches
- No dia: all-day engagement, responder todo comentário
- Depois: follow up, converter tráfego em email

# Email Drip Campaign (Maven style)

## Performance data real
- "1 spot just opened" = 55% open, 4.6% click (melhor)
- "Join 500 marketers" = 49% open, 1.8% click (pior)
- Workshop follow-ups: 54-66% open, 12-21% click (3-4x melhor que all leads)

## Insights
- Scarcity > social proof
- Shorter subjects > longer
- Workshop attendees convertem 3-4x mais
- 4-5% click rate é sólido pra all leads

## Templates
1. Course Promo: hook + social proof + format + scarcity + CTA (80-120 palavras)
2. Workshop Follow-Up: thanks + resources + time limit + course pitch (120-180)
3. Workshop Reminder: timing + coverage + prep + join link (60-100)
